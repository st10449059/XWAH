Life Skills

Life skills are essential abilities that help individuals navigate daily challenges and interactions effectively. The classes aim to empower individuals with the tools they need to lead fulfilling and independent lives.

Concepts covered in the life skills class:

  1. Decision-Making and Problem-Solving:
    • Strategies for making informed decisions and tackling everyday problems.

    1. Time Management:
      • Prioritizing tasks, setting goals, and organizing schedules.

      1. Financial Literacy:
        • Budgeting, saving, managing expenses, and understanding basic financial concepts.

        1. Health and Wellness:
          • Nutrition, exercise, mental health awareness, and stress management.

          1. Interpersonal Skills:
            • Building healthy relationships, teamwork, and empathy.

            1. Critical Thinking:
              • Analysing information, evaluating sources, and making reasoned judgments.

              1. Self-Management:
                • Setting personal goals, self-discipline, and emotional regulation.

                1. Basic Cooking and Household Management:
                  • Meal preparation, cleaning, and other essential home skills.

                  1. Career Skills:
                    • Job searching, resume writing, interview techniques, and workplace etiquette.
